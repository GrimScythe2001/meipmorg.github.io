Let Me Be Your Guardian Angel
- Miegrat Sammri


Oi child,  
Let me guide you,  
Let me guard you,  
Let me be your  
Angel in the afterlife.  

I have seen  
Your yesterdays and tomorrows,  
I have known you  
Closer than the rest of them all.  

I will praise you  
For your good deeds,  
And I will bear the weight  
Of your crimes.
