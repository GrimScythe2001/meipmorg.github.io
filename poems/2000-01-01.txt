Happy New Year
- Miegrat Sammri


Yet another uneventful year fades and unfolds,
Bleeding into a hollow tale, where no brighter memories will again be told.
Indeed, it's a sad, sad life for me to walk in my own shoes,
I only pray I won't have to follow this year's end through.
