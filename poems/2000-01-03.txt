Euthanise Me
- Miegrat Sammri


I'm a dead soul caged in a beating corpse,
Dying by the minute like a sickened horse.
I'm a dead weight, void of all purpose,
Pull the trigger through my eyes,
And put me down. I promise you no remorse.
